CONFERENCE REGISTRATION

Registration

Authors should register their paper by November 8th, 2024. If the registration is not received in time, the paper will not be included in the conference program and proceedings. There should be at least one full registration per paper. Students (as non-authors or as additional authors) can benefit from a discount. Discounts are also available for ACM or IFIP members.

Note that the early registration for the conference ends October 31st, 2024.

The registration link is to be announced.

What Is Included In The Registration

Full conference registrations include participation in the main Middleware program and pre-conference program. Coffee breaks and lunches are provided, as well as one reception and one banquet dinner.

Cancellation Policy

Note that no cancellation and no refunds are possible.

The Middleware conference adheres strictly to the ACM policies against discrimination and harassment.
